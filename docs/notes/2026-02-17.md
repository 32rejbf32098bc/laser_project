# Laser plane calibration improvement

## Objective

- Fix laser plane calibration accuracy
- Improve mechanical stability of system

---

## Laser filtering implementation

### Method

Applied filtering to calibration images:

- Isolated red laser pixels
- Removed checkerboard pattern
- Removed reflections and background noise

---

### Calibration result

- Calibration error dramatically reduced
- Laser plane visually correct
- Plane orientation stable
- Calibration now usable for triangulation

---

## Mechanical improvements

### Problem

Temporary mounts allowed:

- Camera movement
- Laser movement
- Vibration

This affected calibration stability

---

### Solution

Designed and 3D printed new rigid mounts for:

- Camera
- Laser module

---

### Result

- Significantly improved rigidity
- Laser and camera fixed rigidly relative to each other
- Improved system stability

---

## Conclusion

- Laser plane calibration successful
- Mechanical stability significantly improved
- System ready for full scan pipeline testing
