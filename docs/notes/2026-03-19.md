---

## 3. Major Improvement — Red-Excess Image

### Change
Replaced raw red channel:
red = img[:, :, 2]

With:
redish = r - max(g, b)

### Result
- Massive improvement in ridge quality
- Suppressed:
  - reflections
  - non-laser bright edges
- Laser stripe became the dominant detected feature

### Insight
- Raw red channel is insufficient in reflective environments
- Red-excess acts as a simple but highly effective spectral filter

### Impact
- Significantly improves robustness for:
  - video-based scanning
  - real-world deployment (pipes, metal surfaces, etc.)
